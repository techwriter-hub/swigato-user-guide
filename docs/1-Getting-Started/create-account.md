# How to Create a Swigato Account

Welcome to Swigato! To start ordering your favorite meals, you need to create a quick, free account. We use mobile verification to keep your account secure.

## Sign Up with a Mobile Number

1. Open the **Swigato app** on your device.
2. On the welcome screen, tap **Continue with Phone Number**.
3. Enter your 10-digit mobile number and tap **Send OTP**.
4. Check your SMS messages for a 6-digit verification code.
5. Enter the code into the app and tap **Verify**.
6. Type in your first and last name, then tap **Create Account**.

> **Note:** If you don't receive your OTP immediately, wait 30 seconds and tap **Resend Code**.
