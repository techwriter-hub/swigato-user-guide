# How to Set Your Delivery Address

Before you can place an order, Swigato needs to know where to bring your food. You can use your device's built-in GPS to find your location instantly, or you can enter it manually.

## Add a New Address

1. Open the **Swigato app** and ensure you are logged in.
2. Tap your location pin at the very top of the home screen, or go to **Profile** > **Addresses**.
3. Tap **Add New Address**.
4. When the app asks for location permissions, tap **Allow While Using App** to let Swigato find you automatically.
5. Drag the map pin to your exact building and tap **Confirm Location**.
6. Enter your specific flat or floor number, plus a helpful landmark.
7. Select a tag for this location (like **Home** or **Work**) and tap **Save Address**.

> **Note:** If you are ordering for a friend, you can use the search bar at the top of the map to enter their address manually instead of using your device's current location.
