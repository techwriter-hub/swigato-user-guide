# How to Add a Payment Method

To make checkout faster, you can securely save your preferred payment methods to your Swigato profile before you even start looking at menus.

## Link a Card or UPI

1. Open the **Swigato app** and tap the **Profile** icon in the bottom right corner.
2. Tap **Payments & Refunds**, then select **Payment Modes**.
3. Tap **Add New Card** or **Add New UPI ID**.
4. Enter your valid payment details.
5. Tap **Save Securely**.

> **Note:** Swigato may temporarily charge a refundable amount of ₹1 to verify your card details. You can also skip this step entirely and choose **Cash on Delivery (COD)** when you eventually place an order.
