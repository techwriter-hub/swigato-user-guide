# How to Place an Order

Once you have found the perfect meal, you are just a few taps away from getting it delivered.

## Complete Your Checkout

1. Tap **Add** next to your preferred item.
2. Customize your item (e.g., adjust the quantity or select add-ons) and tap **Add to Cart**.
3. Tap the **Cart** icon at the bottom of your screen.
4. Confirm or select your **Delivery Address**.
5. Choose your preferred **Payment Method** (Card, UPI, or Cash on Delivery).
6. Enter any required payment details and tap **Pay Now** (or **Place Order** if using COD).

> **Note:** Once your order is successfully placed, you will automatically be redirected to a live tracking screen so you can watch your food arrive!
