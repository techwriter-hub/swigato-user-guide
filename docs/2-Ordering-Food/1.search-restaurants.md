# How to Search for Restaurants and Food

Craving something specific? You can use Swigato's search and filter tools to find exactly what you want to eat in seconds.

## Find and Filter Results

1. Open the **Swigato app** and tap the **Search** icon (🔍︎) on your home screen.
2. Type the name of a restaurant (e.g.,"Royal Place") or a specific dish (e.g., "Biryani") into the search bar.
3. Tap the **Filters** button to narrow down your options.
4. Select your preferences, such as **Customer Ratings**, **Price**, or **Delivery Time**, and tap **Apply**.
5. Tap on your preferred restaurant from the results list to open their menu.
