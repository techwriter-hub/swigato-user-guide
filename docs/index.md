# Welcome to the Swigato Help Center

Hungry? You are in the right place. The Swigato Help Center is designed to get you from downloading the app to enjoying your first meal as quickly as possible.

## How to Use This Guide

We have organized our guides based on what you want to achieve today:

* **[Getting Started](1-Getting-Started/create-account.md):** Learn how to set up your account, add a delivery address, and link a payment method securely.
* **Ordering Food:** Discover how to search for your favorite local restaurants, customize your dishes, and place an order.
* **Managing Orders:** Find out how to track your delivery partner in real-time or get help with a current order.

If you are new here, we recommend starting with **How to Create an Account**.
